The Indian Premier League (IPL) started in 2008 and has become the most followed T20 league in the world. From 2008 to 2025, a total of 18 seasons have been played.

Mumbai Indians and Chennai Super Kings are the most successful teams, each winning 5 titles. Kolkata Knight Riders have won 3 titles. Rajasthan Royals, Deccan Chargers, Sunrisers Hyderabad, Gujarat Titans, and Royal Challengers Bengaluru have each won 1 title.

Royal Challengers Bengaluru won their first IPL title in 2025 by defeating Punjab Kings by 6 runs at Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad.

IPL Finals History:

In 2008, Rajasthan Royals defeated Chennai Super Kings at DY Patil Stadium in Mumbai.

In 2009, Deccan Chargers defeated Royal Challengers Bangalore at Wanderers Stadium in Johannesburg.

In 2010, Chennai Super Kings defeated Mumbai Indians at DY Patil Stadium in Mumbai.

In 2011, Chennai Super Kings defeated Royal Challengers Bangalore at MA Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai.

In 2012, Kolkata Knight Riders defeated Chennai Super Kings at MA Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai.

In 2013, Mumbai Indians defeated Chennai Super Kings at Eden Gardens in Kolkata.

In 2014, Kolkata Knight Riders defeated Kings XI Punjab at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bangalore.

In 2015, Mumbai Indians defeated Chennai Super Kings at Eden Gardens in Kolkata.

In 2016, Sunrisers Hyderabad defeated Royal Challengers Bangalore at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bangalore.

In 2017, Mumbai Indians defeated Rising Pune Supergiant at Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium in Hyderabad.

In 2018, Chennai Super Kings defeated Sunrisers Hyderabad at Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai.

In 2019, Mumbai Indians defeated Chennai Super Kings at Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium in Hyderabad.

In 2020, Mumbai Indians defeated Delhi Capitals at Dubai International Cricket Stadium.

In 2021, Chennai Super Kings defeated Kolkata Knight Riders at Dubai International Cricket Stadium.

In 2022, Gujarat Titans defeated Rajasthan Royals at Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad.

In 2023, Chennai Super Kings defeated Gujarat Titans at Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad.

In 2024, Kolkata Knight Riders defeated Sunrisers Hyderabad at MA Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai.

In 2025, Royal Challengers Bengaluru defeated Punjab Kings at Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad.

The 2026 IPL season winner and runner-up are yet to be decided.
